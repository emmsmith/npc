Noah's Pet Clinic

We love pets, but more than that we love happy, healthy pets.

We are a veterinary office in rural Manchester. We are a specialised dog clinic but also have other types of pets.

We are here for you every step of the way, from guiding you on the best care for your new kitten or puppy, making things better when things go wrong through to helping you to keep your pet in tip top condition in their senior years.

Care is at the heart of everything we do - looking after you, your pets and your wallet. We aim to give you the best service possible at all times, being realistic about outcomes and offering genuine help and advice for the benefit of pets and their owners.
